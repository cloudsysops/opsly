# Opsly Pilot Program

We're working with a small group of early customers to validate the autonomous DevOps agent in real production environments.

## What you get

- 90-day pilot at reduced rate ($99/mo vs standard $299/mo)
- Direct access to founder for setup and questions
- Influence on roadmap priorities
- "Pay only if it saves you 5+ hours/month" guarantee

## Who we're looking for

- Solo technical founders or small teams (2-10 engineers)
- Running 5-15 services in production (VPS, Docker, n8n, databases)
- Currently spending 5+ hours/week on ops tasks you wish were automated
- Comfortable with command-line and reading agent logs

## Who we're NOT looking for (yet)

- Enterprise customers needing SOC2 / specific compliance certifications
- Teams running >50 services or complex Kubernetes setups (later phase)
- Non-technical founders who need a polished GUI (later phase)

## What we ask in return

- 30 minutes of feedback every 2 weeks for 90 days
- Permission to use anonymized incident data to improve the agent
- Honest review at end of pilot (positive or negative)

## How to apply

Email hello@opsly.dev with:
- Your stack (services, hosting, current ops pain)
- Hours/week you currently lose to ops
- One specific incident you wish hadn't required your attention

We respond within 48 hours.
